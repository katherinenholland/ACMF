About:

The Atlanta Chamber Music Festival is a five day residential music festival for 6-12 grade students who have been playing at least one year. The primary focus is on chamber groups coached by renowned professional musicians from the Atlanta area. We are the only program in Atlanta to offer chamber music, full orchestra including strings, woodwinds, and brass, and elective classes and seminars.

In addition to working with our exceptional faculty in masterclasses, studio class, chamber groups, and large ensembles, students also will have the opportunity to attend our faculty recital, perform in concerts, and participate in group social activities.

The Philosophy

At ACMF, we believe in each student's potential to grow and discover his/her voice through music. Every child has the potential to create through the medium of music.Discover Your Potential

The Difference

ACMF is the only festival of its kind for students in the Atlanta area. We offer chamber music, full orchestra including strings, woodwinds, and brass, and elective classes and seminars in a fun, inspiring, and challenging environment.

In addition to our curriculum, our faculty represent the top musicians in the Atlanta area. They are renowned music educators and active performers as soloists, orchestral, chamber, and touring musicians.
